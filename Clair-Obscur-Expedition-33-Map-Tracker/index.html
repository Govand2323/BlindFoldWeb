Explore the world of Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 in a smarter and more organized way with MapWizard, a companion app built to help you discover locations, track collectibles, and follow your progress with ease.

Whether you are uncovering hidden areas, revisiting locations, or aiming for full completion, MapWizard provides a clean and intuitive interactive map designed specifically for Clair Obscur: Expedition 33.

Key Features

Interactive Map

Navigate the world with smooth zooming and clear, detailed map markers.

Collectible and Location Tracking

Mark items as Found or Not Found and always know what you have already discovered.

Progress Overview

View completion percentages by section and track your overall map progress at a glance.

Search and Filters

Quickly find locations by name and hide or show found items on the map.

Smart Pin Focus

Tap any item to instantly focus on its exact location.

YouTube Video Guides

Watch helpful videos linked directly to specific locations and items.

Offline Support

Once the data is loaded, the map remains accessible even without an internet connection.

Live Content Updates

New locations, data, and guides can be added without requiring an app update.

Designed for Completion and Exploration

MapWizard is ideal for:

  • Players aiming for 100 percent completion
  • Collectible and lore hunters
  • Explorers who do not want to miss hidden content
  • Players tracking their journey through Expedition 33
  • Fans revisiting locations and discoveries
