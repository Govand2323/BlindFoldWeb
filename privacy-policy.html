PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: June 13, 2026

At BlindFold Games ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal data when you use our games and apps, which are available on the Google Play Store and the iOS App Store.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from users of our games. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or other personal details.

2. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect personal data, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements within our games. AdMob may collect certain information in order to provide personalized ads. This includes:

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Advertising ID
  • Location information (if enabled)

These third-party services may use cookies, web beacons, and other technologies to collect data and display ads that are more relevant to you. The data collected by AdMob is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, which you can review here: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

3. How We Use the Information

The information collected by AdMob is used to:

  • Display ads that are tailored to your interests.
  • Improve the overall experience of our games.

We do not have access to this information and do not use it for any other purpose.

4. Your Choices

Opt-out of Personalized Ads: If you would like to limit the personalized ads you see, you can do so by changing the settings on your device. For more details on how to opt out of personalized ads, visit Google's Ad Settings.

Limit Ad Tracking: You can also limit ad tracking by adjusting your device settings. This will prevent AdMob from collecting your advertising ID.

5. Data Security

We do not store or process any personal data. As a result, your privacy is safeguarded. However, AdMob and other third-party services may have their own security measures in place for data they collect.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child, we will take steps to remove that information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us at: mapwizard.gg@gmail.com
