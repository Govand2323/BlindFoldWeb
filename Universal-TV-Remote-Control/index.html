Universal TV Remote Control turns your phone into a remote for TVs on your home Wi-Fi network. There's no account, no login, and no cloud — remote commands go directly from your phone to your TV on your local network. It works with popular TV types including Roku, Samsung, LG, Fire TV, Android/Google TV, and Hisense/VIDAA.
