Explore the vast world of Red Dead Redemption 2 like never before with MapWizard — a powerful companion app designed to help you discover locations, track collectibles, and monitor your progress with ease.

Whether you're hunting legendary animals, uncovering hidden secrets, or aiming for 100% completion, MapWizard provides a clean, intuitive, and interactive map built specifically for the world of Red Dead Redemption 2.

⭐ Key Features

🗺️ Interactive RDR2 Map

Navigate the massive open world with smooth zooming, precise controls, and clearly organized map pins.

📍 Custom Pins (NEW)

Create your own markers anywhere on the map. Add titles, notes, icons, and colors to remember important locations like loot spots, hunting areas, or secrets you discover.

🐾 Animal Heatmaps (NEW)

Find animals faster with visual heatmaps showing where each species is most likely to spawn. Perfect for hunting, crafting, and completing compendium entries.

📦 Collectible & Location Tracker

Mark items as Found / Not Found and keep track of everything you've discovered across the map.

📊 Progress Overview

See your completion progress by category and track how close you are to 100%.

🔍 Search & Filter

Quickly find locations by name and filter the map to show exactly what you need.

🎯 Smart Pin Focus

Tap any item to instantly center the map on its exact location.

🎥 YouTube Guides

Access helpful video guides linked directly to specific locations and collectibles.

📶 Offline Support

Once loaded, the map remains fully usable even without an internet connection.

🔄 Live Content Updates

New locations, guides, and improvements can be added without updating the app.

🎯 Built for Explorers & Completionists

MapWizard is perfect for:

  • Players aiming for 100% completion
  • Hunters tracking animals and rare spawns
  • Collectible and treasure hunters
  • Explorers who don't want to miss hidden content
  • Players revisiting areas and tracking discoveries
