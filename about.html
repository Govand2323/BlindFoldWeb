About Us

WHO WE ARE

BlindFold Games is a small studio building games and apps with passion. We love creating tools that make life easier and more enjoyable to use, and games that are simply fun to play.

We're a tiny team that cares about the little details — smooth experiences, clean design, and that satisfying feeling when something just works. Whether it's a quick game to pass the time or an app that solves a real problem, we put the same heart into everything we ship.

What we believe

  • Fun first. If it isn't enjoyable, it isn't done.
  • Easy to use. Great tools should feel effortless.
  • Made with care. Quality over quantity, always.
