MapWizard: GTAV Map & Tracker is a powerful interactive companion app for Grand Theft Auto V, designed to help you explore the world, track collectibles, and complete everything with ease.

Navigate detailed GTA V maps with precise pins for collectibles, locations, and points of interest. Tap any pin to see full details, descriptions, and helpful YouTube guides when available.

🔍 Interactive Map

  • Smooth zoom and pan controls
  • Clear, color-coded pins
  • Focus instantly on any location

✅ Tracker & Progress System

  • Mark items as Found / Not Found
  • Track progress by element, section, and map
  • Visual progress bars and percentages
  • Overall completion progress at a glance

🔎 Smart Search

  • Search locations instantly by name
  • Automatically hides unrelated pins while searching

🎯 Clean Legend System

  • Show or hide entire categories
  • Toggle elements individually or all at once
  • Instantly update the map view

📺 YouTube Integration

  • Watch related guide videos directly
  • Automatic video thumbnails for quick reference

🔄 Live Content Updates

New locations, collectibles, and videos can be added remotely — no app update required. Your map stays fresh even after release.

🌍 Built for Players

  • Fast and lightweight
  • Works offline after first load
  • Saves progress automatically

Whether you're aiming for 100% completion or just exploring Los Santos, MapWizard gives you full control over your GTA V experience.
